# Product Limitations

- Lexora is not legal, tax, accounting, or payment-processing advice.
- Invoice totals, GST treatment, write-offs, refunds, and payment reconciliation must be reviewed by the workspace before external filing or collection.
- Gmail, research, web, and desktop capture can miss activity if permissions, connectivity, browser policy, or device state prevents capture.
- Offline capture is queued and retried, but duplicate-source protection may exclude repeated events.
- PDF reports are not offered as a general report format; CSV/dashboard reporting is supported.
