# Data Export And Deletion

## Export
Workspace owners may request exports of:
- Clients, matters, contacts, tasks, work entries, billables, expenses, invoices, payments, receipts, audit events, and report snapshots.
- Stored documents and generated invoice PDFs.

Target response time: 10 business days for pilot customers.

## Deletion
Workspace owners may request deletion after identity and authority verification.

Deletion excludes records that must be retained for legal, billing, fraud-prevention, security, or audit purposes. Where hard deletion is unsafe, records are quarantined or anonymized.

## Procedure
1. Verify requester is a workspace Owner.
2. Freeze new integrations for the workspace.
3. Generate export package if requested.
4. Confirm deletion scope.
5. Delete or quarantine data.
6. Confirm completion and retention exceptions.
