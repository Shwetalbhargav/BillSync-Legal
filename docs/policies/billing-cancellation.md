# Billing And Cancellation

Plans are billed according to the active order form or pilot agreement. The initial small-firm plan supports up to five active members.

Customers may cancel by contacting `billing@lexora.example`. Access remains available through the paid period unless the account is suspended for security or material breach.

After cancellation, customers may request export and deletion under the data export and deletion workflow.

Refunds and credits are handled explicitly and are not created by editing historical invoices or payments.
