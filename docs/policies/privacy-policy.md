# Privacy Policy

Lexora processes customer, matter, time, billing, payment, document, Gmail capture, research capture, and optional desktop capture data to provide legal billing and practice-management services.

We collect only data needed to deliver the service, secure accounts, maintain audit records, support customers, and meet legal obligations. Capture features require explicit user consent and can be disabled by workspace administrators.

Customer data is logically scoped by workspace. Production secrets must be stored in a managed secret service. Backups are encrypted and retained according to the backup policy.

Customers may request export or deletion of their workspace data subject to legal, billing, fraud-prevention, and audit-retention requirements.

Contact: `privacy@lexora.example`.
