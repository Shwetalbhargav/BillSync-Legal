# BillSync Legal: Lawyer User Testing Guide

**Objective:** This guide is designed to help you explore the BillSync Legal workspace and provide feedback on how intuitively it supports your daily legal workflows. No technical knowledge is required.

---

## 1. 30-Minute Testing Script

*   **0-5 mins:** Introduction and Login.
*   **5-15 mins:** Daily Workflow (Matters, Tasks, and Time Capture).
*   **15-25 mins:** Intelligent Capture & Review (Chrome Extension & AI).
*   **25-30 mins:** Wrap-up and Feedback.

---

## 2. Step-by-Step Tasks

### Task 1: Secure Access & Setup
*   **Action:** Log in and check if your workspace is ready.
*   **What to click:** Enter credentials on the **Login** screen, then click **My Setup Status** in the sidebar.
*   **What you should see:** A clear progress bar showing how much of your firm setup is complete.
*   **Verify:** Do you understand which tools are connected and which "Need Update"?
*   **Feedback:** Does this status page make you feel confident that your work is being tracked?

### Task 2: Matter & Task Management
*   **Action:** Find your priority work for the day.
*   **What to click:** Click **Dashboard**, then click on an active matter like "Harrison Acquisition."
*   **What you should see:** The **Matter Detail** view showing financial summaries and upcoming milestones.
*   **Verify:** Can you clearly see the "Next Steps" for this case?
*   **Feedback:** Is the matter summary density appropriate, or is it too much information at once?

### Task 3: Precision Time Capture
*   **Action:** Record an active work session.
*   **What to click:** Click **Start Work** on your dashboard or within a task. After a few moments, click **Stop Work**.
*   **What you should see:** A pulsing recording indicator while active; a notes field appearing once stopped.
*   **Verify:** Enter a work note and click **Submit for Billing**.
*   **Feedback:** Does the "Work Meter" feel reliable? Was it clear that your time was successfully saved?

### Task 4: Automated Capture Review
*   **Action:** Verify work captured from Gmail and legal research.
*   **What to click:** Click **Chrome Extension Status** in settings, then navigate to **Captured Work**.
*   **What you should see:** Items listed as "Found in Gmail" or "Found in Westlaw."
*   **Verify:** Review an entry and click **Confirm as Billable**.
*   **Feedback:** Does the "Gmail Capture Working" status give you peace of mind?

### Task 5: Manual Entry & AI Assistance
*   **Action:** Record a court hearing and use the AI helper.
*   **What to click:** Click **Manual Entry**, select "Court Hearing." Then, click **AI Assistant**.
*   **What you should see:** A specialized form for court details; a chat interface for the AI.
*   **Verify:** Ask the AI to "Summarize my work for today."
*   **Feedback:** Did the court entry form include the fields you expect (Judge, Room, etc.)?

---

## 3. Handling Exceptions (Fallback States)

Please review the following scenarios and note your reaction:
*   **Scenario A (No Matters):** "You are not assigned to any matters yet." Is the instruction to "Ask Admin" clear?
*   **Scenario B (Save Failed):** If a timer fails to save, is the "Retry Save" button and the reassurance that "Your time is not lost" helpful?
*   **Scenario C (Extension Offline):** If the extension isn't connected, does the setup guide feel manageable?
