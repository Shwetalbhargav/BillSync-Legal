Quick Guided Setup

Follow these 8 steps to enable BillSync for your browser. This one-time setup ensures your billable hours are captured with bank-grade precision.

1

Download BillSync Chrome Extension

Get the latest beta package specifically configured for your firm.

2

Open Chrome Extensions

Type chrome://extensions in your address bar or click the puzzle icon.

open_in_new
3

Turn on Developer Mode

Toggle the switch in the top right corner of the Extensions page to enable testing.

4

Click Load Unpacked

This button appears in the top left once Developer Mode is active.

Load Unpacked
5

Select the BillSync Extension folder

Locate the unzipped folder from Step 1 in your file browser and click "Select".

folder_open
6

Click Connect to BillSync

The extension popup will appear. Log in with your LexisPremium credentials.

7

Send a test Gmail message or capture research

Open Gmail or Westlaw to verify that BillSync detects your activity.

mail menu_book
8

Confirm status says 'Working'

Check the extension icon in your toolbar. It should glow gold when active.

check_circle Working

All Finished?

Notify your administrator once you've completed the walkthrough.
